# Overview

在職家庭津貼（職津）鼓勵自力更生，幫助有人在職但收入不高、並非領取綜援的家庭。除
家庭津貼外，另設**兒童津貼**補助在學或年幼子女。 [1]

# Official Purpose

To encourage self-reliance and ease financial pressure on lower-income working
households not receiving CSSA, with an additional child allowance for eligible
children. [1]

# Eligibility

**Objective:** Support low-income working households; supplement children's
costs.
**Who can apply:** Households with a member meeting the monthly **working-hour**
requirement, whose income and assets are within limits, and who are **not** on
CSSA. [1]
**Who cannot apply:** Households receiving **CSSA** (cannot hold both). [1]
**Eligibility criteria:** Working-hour threshold + income limit + asset limit +
not on CSSA. ⚠️ Needs Manual Review (thresholds).
**Household definition:** WFA household as defined by WFAO. See
`glossary.md#household`. ⚠️ Needs Manual Review.
**Income definition:** Household income per WFAO rules (with disregards). ⚠️
Needs Manual Review.
**Assets definition:** Household asset limit applies. ⚠️ Needs Manual Review.
**Residency requirement:** Members generally HK residents ordinarily resident in
HK. ⚠️ Needs Manual Review.

# Means Test

Income **and** asset tested. Allowance is tiered (e.g. basic / medium / higher
levels) by household income and working hours; a separate **child allowance** is
paid per eligible child. Exact hours, limits and amounts published by WFAO —
**⚠️ Needs Manual Review** (quote current figures with source + date). [1]

# Required Documents

**Required:**
- Applicant and household members' HK identity documents
- Income proof

- Bank account details

**Supporting (if applicable):**

# FAQ

**Q:** 攞緊綜援可唔可以同時攞職津？
**A:** 唔可以，綜援同職津不可同時領取。 [1]

**Q:** 冇小朋友可唔可以攞？
**A:** 可以攞家庭津貼部分；兒童津貼則按合資格兒童發放。⚠️ Confirm official wording. [1]
