書簿津貼計劃幫助通過入息審查的清貧中小學日校學生，支付書簿及部分學習雜費。合資格
學生可獲**全額或半額**資助。 [1][2]

# Official Purpose

To provide financial assistance towards the cost of textbooks and miscellaneous
study-related expenses for needy primary and secondary day-school students who
pass the means test. [2]

# Eligibility

**Objective:** Offset textbook / study expenses for low-income students.
**Who can apply:** Parents/guardians of students attending a local primary or
secondary **day school**, whose household passes the means test. [2]
**Who cannot apply:** Households currently receiving **CSSA** — school-related
needs are covered under CSSA, so no duplicate application. [2] ⚠️ Confirm exact
exclusion wording.
**Eligibility criteria:** Local day-school primary/secondary student + household
passes SFO means test. [2]
**Household definition:** As defined by SFO for the means test — see
`glossary.md#household`. ⚠️ Needs Manual Review.
**Income definition:** SFO adjusted family income. ⚠️ Needs Manual Review

**Residency requirement:** Student generally a HK resident. ⚠️ Needs Manual

# Required Documents

**Required:**
- Applicant (parent/guardian) HK identity document
- Student's identity document / birth certificate
- Proof of student status (school-issued)
- Address proof
- Income proof for household members
- Bank account details (for disbursement)

**Supporting (if applicable):**
- CSSA documents (if relevant), tenancy documents, etc. ⚠️ Confirm official

# Application Flow

- **Application window:** Annual, during the school year. ⚠️ Needs Manual Review
  (confirm current dates). [2]
- **How to apply / submission channel:** Combined "Student Finance Scheme"
  application form; submit via **SFO E-link** online or by post. One combined
  application can cover multiple SFO student schemes at once. [1][2]
- **Review process:** SFO checks documents and assesses household means. [2]

# Approval Flow

- **Approval workflow:** Result issued by notification; assistance level (full/
  half) stated. [2]
- **Additional information requests:** SFO may request further documents if the
  application is incomplete. ⚠️ Confirm official wording.
- **Appeal:** ⚠️ Needs Manual Review (confirm appeal mechanism / window).

# Payment

Generally paid by **autopay** into the nominated bank account. ⚠️ Confirm timing
and instalment arrangement. [2]

# Renewal

Typically re-apply each school year; continuing applicants may use a simplified
process but are re-assessed. ⚠️ Needs Manual Review. [2]

# Exceptions

- Households on **CSSA** should not double-apply (covered by CSSA). [2]
- Overpayment recovery / cancellation on change of circumstances. ⚠️ Needs
  Manual Review.
