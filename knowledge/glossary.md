# 政府津貼詞彙表 · Government Subsidy Glossary

> Seed draft. Definitions below are aligned to how the terms are used across
> official WFSFAA / SWD documents, but the **verbatim official wording and any
> figures must be confirmed** by kb-terminology-expert against the cited pages.
> Items not yet confirmed are marked `⚠️ Needs Manual Review`.

## Means Test / 入息審查（資產審查）

- **Official definition:** The assessment the government uses to decide whether a
  household qualifies for assistance and at what level (e.g. full or half rate),
  based on household income and, for some schemes, assets. [1][2]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 政府睇你屋企賺幾多錢（有時仲睇有幾多資產），再決定可唔可以攞資助、攞幾多。
- **Simple English explanation:** A check of your household's income (and
  sometimes assets) to decide if you qualify and how much you get.
- **Common misunderstanding:** People assume only the applicant's income counts —
  most means tests count the **whole household**.
- **Related subsidies:** textbook_assistance, travel_subsidy, internet_subsidy,
  kindergarten_fee_remission, working_family_allowance, community_care_service_voucher.
- **Official source:** [1] WFSFAA Student Finance; [2] SWD Social Security.

## Household / 家庭（住戶）

- **Official definition:** The group of people whose income and members are
  counted together for the means test — typically those living together and
  sharing living expenses; the exact composition rules differ by scheme. ⚠️ Needs
  Manual Review (confirm each scheme's household definition). [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 一齊住、一齊分擔開支嘅一家人；審查時當一個單位計。
- **Simple English explanation:** The people counted together as one family unit
  for the income check.
- **Common misunderstanding:** That "household" always equals the people on the
  tenancy — scheme rules may include/exclude certain relatives.
- **Related subsidies:** all means-tested schemes.
- **Official source:** [1] WFSFAA / SWD application guides.

## Eligible Household / 合資格住戶

- **Official definition:** A household that meets all the scheme's conditions
  (income/assets within limits, residency, student/age status, etc.) and is
  therefore entitled to apply. [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 符合晒條件、可以攞資助嗰戶人。
- **Simple English explanation:** A family that meets all the rules and can get
  the subsidy.
- **Common misunderstanding:** Being low-income alone makes a household eligible —
  other conditions (residency, student status) also apply.
- **Related subsidies:** internet_subsidy, community_care_service_voucher.
- **Official source:** [1] scheme application guides.

## Dependent Child / 受養子女

- **Official definition:** A child financially supported by the applicant who
  meets the scheme's age / schooling conditions (e.g. under a set age, or in
  full-time education). ⚠️ Needs Manual Review (confirm age/schooling cut-off per
  scheme). [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 由申請人供養、又符合年齡或讀書條件嘅仔女。
- **Simple English explanation:** A child you support who meets the age/school
  rules.
- **Common misunderstanding:** All children count — the age/full-time-study
  condition matters (esp. for Working Family Allowance child element).
- **Related subsidies:** working_family_allowance.
- **Official source:** [1] WFAO guide.

## Continuing Student / Continuing Applicant · 舊生 / 繼續申請人

- **Official definition:** An applicant who applied in a previous cycle and is
  re-applying, often via a simplified process. [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 之前申請過、今年再申請嘅人。
- **Simple English explanation:** Someone who applied before and is applying
  again.
- **Common misunderstanding:** That continuing applicants skip the means test —
  they are usually still re-assessed.
- **Related subsidies:** textbook_assistance, travel_subsidy, internet_subsidy.
- **Official source:** [1] SFO application guide.

## Student Applicant / 學生申請人

- **Official definition:** The student on whose behalf the assistance is applied
  for (as opposed to the parent/guardian who signs). [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 資助係為佢而申請嘅嗰個學生。
- **Simple English explanation:** The student the help is for.
- **Common misunderstanding:** Confusing the student-applicant with the
  applicant/guardian who submits the form.
- **Related subsidies:** all SFO student schemes.
- **Official source:** [1] SFO.

## Full Grant / Half Grant · 全額 / 半額資助

- **Official definition:** Two levels of assistance under means-tested student
  schemes; the household's assessed means determines full vs half. Exact income
  bands are published each school year. ⚠️ Needs Manual Review (current-year
  bands). [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 全額即攞足；半額即攞一半，睇你屋企收入。
- **Simple English explanation:** Full = you get the whole amount; half = you get
  50%, depending on income.
- **Common misunderstanding:** That the level is fixed — it depends on the annual
  means-test result.
- **Related subsidies:** textbook_assistance, travel_subsidy.
- **Official source:** [1] SFO adjusted family income notes.

## Household Income / 住戶入息

- **Official definition:** The combined income of all counted household members
  used in the means test; some schemes use an **adjusted** family income formula.
  ⚠️ Needs Manual Review (confirm formula per scheme). [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 屋企所有計算成員嘅收入加埋一齊。
- **Simple English explanation:** Everyone in the household's income added up.
- **Common misunderstanding:** Only wages count — other income (allowances, some
  benefits) may be included; some items are disregarded.
- **Related subsidies:** all means-tested schemes.
- **Official source:** [1] SFO / WFAO.

## Gross Income / 總收入 · Net Income / 淨收入

- **Official definition:** Gross income is income before mandatory deductions;
  net income is after allowable deductions (e.g. MPF). Which one a scheme uses is
  scheme-specific. ⚠️ Needs Manual Review. [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 總收入係未扣嘢之前；淨收入係扣咗（例如強積金）之後。
- **Simple English explanation:** Gross = before deductions; net = after allowed
  deductions.
- **Common misunderstanding:** Assuming the scheme uses gross when it uses net (or
  vice versa).
- **Related subsidies:** working_family_allowance, SFO schemes.
- **Official source:** [1] scheme guides.

## Supporting Documents / 證明文件

- **Official definition:** Documents required to prove the statements in an
  application (identity, address, income, student status, etc.). [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 用嚟證明你填嘅嘢係真嘅文件。
- **Simple English explanation:** Proof you attach to back up your application.
- **Common misunderstanding:** Copies are always fine — some documents must be
  originals or certified.
- **Related subsidies:** all.
- **Official source:** [1] scheme document checklists.

## Residence / Ordinarily Resident · 居港 / 通常居於香港

- **Official definition:** A residency condition requiring the applicant/student
  to be a Hong Kong resident and (for many schemes) ordinarily resident in Hong
  Kong; specific year requirements vary (e.g. CSSA residence rules, OALA/OAA
  residence). ⚠️ Needs Manual Review (confirm per scheme). [1][2]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 要係香港居民、平時住喺香港；唔同計劃對居港年期要求唔同。
- **Simple English explanation:** You must be a HK resident normally living in HK;
  the required years differ by scheme.
- **Common misunderstanding:** That a HK ID alone always satisfies residency — some
  schemes need a minimum number of years ordinarily resident.
- **Related subsidies:** old_age_living_allowance, old_age_allowance,
  cssa_student_support, disability_allowance.
- **Official source:** [1] SWD Social Security; [2] scheme guides.

## iAM Smart / iAM Smart+ · 智方便 / 智方便+

- **Official definition:** The government's single digital identity app for
  logging in to and (with iAM Smart+) digitally signing online government forms.
  [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 政府嘅手機應用，用嚟登入同網上簽名，慳返填表時間。
- **Simple English explanation:** A government app to log in and e-sign online
  forms.
- **Common misunderstanding:** That it is required — it is optional, an
  alternative to paper.
- **Related subsidies:** SFO e-applications.
- **Official source:** [1] iamsmart.gov.hk.

## SFO E-link / 學生資助處電子通

- **Official definition:** The Student Finance Office online platform for
  submitting and tracking student finance applications. [1]
- **簡單中文解釋:** 學生資助處嘅網上系統，喺度交申請同睇進度。
- **Simple English explanation:** SFO's website for submitting and checking
  student-finance applications.
- **Common misunderstanding:** Confusing it with the general WFSFAA site.
- **Related subsidies:** all SFO student schemes.
- **Official source:** [1] ess.wfsfaa.gov.hk.
